POWER BI DESKTOP | Figma

The Dashboard focuses on the Economic inactivity of Disabled People

I want to shift the focus towards economic inactivity and draw comparisons between disabled and non-disabled people. This subject is often considered taboo, but the insights from these reports could greatly benefit educators and training providers. The goal is to help disabled individuals in returning to work, provide motivation, and serve as role models. The impact of these factors can vary depending on the individual’s region within the UK.

I created this dashboard in Tableau Desktop, which was my first project ever. It was very challenging, but I learned a lot. POWER BI DESKTOP & POWERPOINT

Deaf Together Dashboard

Background Project: Deaf Together is an organisation based in Wales that aims to promote the inclusion of deaf people in society. It is a Deaf-led gathering that’s open to everyone and is part of Hear We Are, a two-year Deaf-led project by Deaf artist, practitioner, and Access Consultant Jonny Cotsen, in collaboration with Chapter who’ve been working with partners to centre the creative voices of Deaf and Hard of Hearing people across Wales. The organisation hosts events, activities, and performances at Chapter, online, and in venues across Wales.

Disabilities Crime Dashboard

Background Project: I translated this dashboard from the Office for National Statistics website to highlight key issues and aims for the police and local government and even training providers to help them understand the day-to-day impact crime has on disabled people.
