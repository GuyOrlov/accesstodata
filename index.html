POWER BI DESKTOP & POWERPOINT | DEAF TOGETHER DASHBOARD
  • Background Project: Deaf Together is an organisation based in Wales that aims to promote the inclusion of deaf people in society. It is a Deaf-led gathering that’s open to everyone and is part of Hear We Are, a two-year Deaf-led project by Deaf artist, practitioner, and Access Consultant Jonny Cotsen, in collaboration with Chapter who’ve been working with partners to centre the creative voices of Deaf and Hard of Hearing people across Wales. The organisation hosts events, activities, and performances at Chapter, online, and in venues across Wales. Some of the events include yoga, comedy, dialogues, and Deaf Rave. In addition to this, Deaf Together also hosts symposiums where they share conversations about why inclusion matters and what people’s hopes are for the future of Deaf Culture in Wales. It will be enhanced through their surveys and local feedback, which will contribute to improving their activities. I am also pleased to be collaborating with Deaf Together in September 2024. The wireframes and design background were developed using PowerPoint slides.
